Channel: Pat Walls (Starter Story)

## Key Ideas

- Joseph (Supademo) grew to $3M ARR and 150,000+ users using a three-layer
  content funnel: bottom-funnel competitor comparison pages, mid-funnel free
  tools with the product embedded, and top-funnel programmatic SEO pages.
- The "do things that don't scale" phase — personally building free demos for
  strangers on Reddit — generated the first wave of paying customers before
  any content engine was running.
- Distribution density beats a single magic channel — SEO/LLM drives 30-40%,
  word of mouth/referrals 30%, and LinkedIn building-in-public 20%.
- YouTube is used as a discovery and trust platform — Starter Story's channel
  is the vehicle through which these founder playbooks reach hundreds of
  thousands of B2B SaaS builders.

## Why It Matters

This video is a direct demonstration of Pat Walls using YouTube to distribute
high-signal B2B SaaS growth playbooks at scale. The channel itself is the
product — it shows how a media-first approach to YouTube can drive both
audience and business revenue simultaneously.
