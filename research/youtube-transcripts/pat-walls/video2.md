# Video Title: I Built 4 SaaS Apps to $100K MRR: Here's My Exact Playbook

Link: https://youtu.be/xeUhKuJbeWQ?si=jCQ9OYM6ubHLSFMi
Date: December 7, 2025
Channel: Pat Walls (Starter Story)

## Key Ideas

- Tibo (Revid.ai, Outrank, SuperX) built four separate SaaS products each
  exceeding $100K/month — $700K total MRR — using the same repeatable
  12-step playbook.
- Step 10 of the playbook is explicit: "At one point, a company needs to
  become a media company." Content creation is not optional at scale — it
  becomes the engine that fuels every other channel.
- Building in public on socials drove Outrank from $0 to $20K/month before
  any paid acquisition — proving organic content compounds into real revenue.
- Tibo's framework separates product-market fit (steps 1-8) from distribution
  (steps 9-12) — YouTube and SEO are specifically named as the sustainable
  long-term acquisition channels worth doubling down on.

## Why It Matters

Pat Walls uses this interview to demonstrate exactly how Starter Story's
YouTube channel operates — surfacing founder playbooks that are directly
actionable for B2B SaaS builders. The channel has become the primary
distribution asset of the Starter Story business itself.
