# Video Title: Start a SaaS From $0 in 2026

Link: https://youtu.be/xJX9uu2HUZ0?si=vBi-bzLOVVcUzCyf
Date: May 24, 2026
Channel: Rob Walling (MicroConf)

## Key Ideas

- The real cost of building a SaaS is not the product — it is finding people
  willing to pay for it. Marketing and distribution are the true expensive part.
- YouTube is explicitly named as the number two search engine and a legitimate
  free channel for early-stage SaaS founders with no marketing budget.
- Content marketing compounds over time — SEO and YouTube together build an
  engine that drives organic traffic month after month.
- Founders should run one fast channel (cold outreach) and one slow channel
  (content/SEO) simultaneously to avoid either starving or always chasing leads.
- Original thinking and proprietary expertise beat AI-generated content — the
  bar for standing out is rising, not falling.

## Why It Matters

Rob directly positions YouTube as a core acquisition channel for bootstrapped
B2B SaaS founders with no budget. His framework of pairing fast and slow
channels gives a practical blueprint for how YouTube fits into a broader SaaS
growth strategy.
