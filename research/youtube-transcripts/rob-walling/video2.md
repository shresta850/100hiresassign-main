# Video Title: Not Sure How to Market Your SaaS? Start Here

Link: https://youtu.be/Hy4eBvxvVfk?si=f1Onf3x7I20aOnPq
Date: March 29, 2026
Channel: Rob Walling (MicroConf)

## Key Ideas

- The "Big Five" B2B SaaS marketing strategies are SEO, pay-per-click ads,
  cold outreach, integration marketing, and content marketing — YouTube sits
  inside both SEO and content marketing simultaneously.
- YouTube is the number two search engine — ranking on YouTube follows the
  same principles as Google: quality content that directly answers questions.
- Content marketing has two paths: virality (short-term spikes) and audience
  building (slow, compounding growth) — Rob recommends audience building for
  long-term SaaS growth.
- Only 10-20% of SaaS founders should build a media brand via YouTube — but
  those who do it well create a durable, scalable acquisition engine.
- AI can help produce content faster but original thinking, proprietary data,
  and real expertise are what actually win in 2026.

## Why It Matters

This video provides a structured framework for where YouTube fits inside the
full B2B SaaS marketing stack. Rob treats YouTube not as a vanity channel but
as a compounding search and audience asset — directly relevant to this
research topic.
