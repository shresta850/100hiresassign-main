# Video title: How to Build a Profitable SaaS from Zero: 5 Lessons

Link: https://youtu.be/Y7j995RddrA?si=DTPO_T02uXMwdH0M
Date: Dec 15, 2025
Channel: Jonathan Rintala (Univid)

## Key Ideas

- Ship fast — same-day/same-night shipping early on, especially with first customers, builds trust and generates word-of-mouth.
- Double down hard on what works and cut the rest; track one North Star metric (for Univid, number of events on the platform) and focus all effort on moving it.
- Dare to fail hard — fearless experimentation pairs with building in public (sharing wins, fails, and screwups openly); references his own separate "10 Ways to Build in Public" video.
- Build it your way — don't chase hypergrowth or investor-driven goals by default; question advisors' and investors' incentives.
- Compound 1% daily improvements — automate workflows (e.g., pre-demo question sequences) so the business can run without the founder, which also increases acquisition value later.

## Why It Matters

General SaaS-building advice, but explicitly references "building in public" and content as part of his growth toolkit. His most recent video (6 months ago), useful for how he frames content/audience-building within his broader growth philosophy.
