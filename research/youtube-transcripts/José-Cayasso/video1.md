# Video Title: How Slidebean Mastered YouTube to Become Their #1 Sales Channel

Link: https://youtu.be/E9twlH_fX3o?si=5-_K_GEBkttBk7rf
Date: January 10, 2021
Channel: Jose "Caya" Cayasso (Slidebean)

## Key Ideas

- Slidebean hit a ceiling with Google SEO and deliberately pivoted to YouTube
  as their next acquisition channel — treating it as the second largest search
  engine, not a brand awareness tool.
- First breakthrough came from replicating their best-performing Google keywords
  on YouTube — starting with "pitch deck" and building from there.
- Keyword-driven content drove the first 30,000 subscribers, after which they
  launched "Company Forensics" (failed startup stories) to expand the top of
  funnel from 30K to 160K subscribers.
- YouTube now pays for itself — ad revenue and sponsorships exceed production
  costs, AND it remains Slidebean's biggest source of product revenue.
- Caya personally hosts all videos — founder face on camera creates trust that
  a hired presenter cannot replicate for a B2B audience.
- Thumbnails are massively underrated — Slidebean uses clean, design-forward
  thumbnails consistent with their brand identity as a presentation tool.

## Why It Matters

This is one of the most detailed real-world case studies of a B2B SaaS company
using YouTube as a primary revenue channel. Caya breaks down the exact sequence
— keywords first, audience second, monetization third — making it a replicable
playbook for any B2B SaaS founder.
