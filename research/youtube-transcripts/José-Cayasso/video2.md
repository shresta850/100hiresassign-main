# Video Title: How We Made $2.5M from YouTube and Google

Link: https://youtu.be/_3_aAxneu2w?si=Ek3fGM9HbSDsPCPi
Date: May 26, 2020
Channel: Jose "Caya" Cayasso (Slidebean)

## Key Ideas

- Slidebean's website gets 250,000 organic visits per month and their YouTube
  channel gets close to 500,000 monthly organic views — combined these have
  generated over $2.5M in sales.
- The journey: paid Google Search ads → organic Google SEO → YouTube, each
  channel unlocked only after the previous one hit its ceiling.
- Content marketing should never be the first strategy — only pursue it after
  unit economics are proven and you know exactly who your audience is.
- Slidebean spent $70,000 over 11 months to build their Google SEO engine
  before it paid off — YouTube followed the same patient, compounding logic.
- Video content must match brand identity — as a design/presentation company,
  Slidebean's videos had to look good or they would undermine the product.

## Why It Matters

This video provides the financial proof behind Slidebean's YouTube strategy —
real numbers ($2.5M in attributed sales) and a clear sequenced framework for
when and how to invest in YouTube as a B2B SaaS growth channel.
