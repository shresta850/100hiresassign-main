# Video title: I Made $1 Million in 2025 [Wrapped]

Link: https://youtu.be/JKFmLdoLm8M?si=nDx1qHcxsrI7gbMJ
Date: January 5, 2026
Channel: Marc Lou (DataFast, ShipFast, CodeFast)

## Key Ideas

- Breaks down 2025 revenue across 15 sources; two products (CodeFast, ShipFast) generated two-thirds of total revenue at ~$20K/month each.
- DataFast reached ~$16K MRR (~$200K ARR) after 200+ days of feature-building and public sharing on Twitter, attributed to users organically sharing product screenshots rather than paid acquisition.
- A new project, Trust Mr (a verified-startup-revenue database), grew from idea to ~$50K MRR within months, largely from one viral tweet referencing another indie hacker.
- States he "never has to do any marketing" for DataFast because "users are actually doing marketing for me" by sharing dashboard screenshots publicly.
- Notes three failed products that same year despite using the same build-in-public approach, underscoring that public content alone doesn't guarantee product-market fit.

## Why It Matters

Provides hard, quantified revenue-by-source numbers — useful evidence for a playbook. Also a useful counterpoint: confirms Marc Lou's primary growth engine is Twitter-driven virality and user-generated sharing, not a deliberate YouTube content strategy, which is worth noting for balance in the final synthesis.
