## Key Ideas

- YouTube is named as the best free channel to reach people, citing 120M+ daily viewers and 1B+ daily watch hours on the platform.

- Short-form reaches more people but has less impact per viewer; long-form builds deeper trust faster.
- Recommends pairing content with "engineering as marketing" (free tools for SEO/backlinks), viral loops, and affiliate marketing as low-cost acquisition channels.
- Free channels trade cash cost for time cost — framed as a deliberate resourcing decision, not a shortcut.
