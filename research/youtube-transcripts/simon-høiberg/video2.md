# Video title: How AI Agents Run My SaaS

## Key Ideas

- Captures raw ideas via voice notes, screenshots, or comments; an AI agent structures these into finished content.
- The agent manages the full production pipeline: graphics (via Remotion), asset prep, and scheduling through FeedHive.
- Designed to remove friction between idea and publication — the founder still originates every idea, the system only handles execution.
- The same system tracks what's already scheduled and selects content format automatically (carousel, quick post, long-form).
- Also used to evaluate paid ad performance, separating genuine signups from low-quality signal.

## Why It Matters

Demonstrates Simon's actual content production system — how a solo/small-team founder sustains a consistent content pipeline at scale. Complements the channel-strategy reasoning with practical execution mechanics.
